### Overview

This repository presents a **first-principles reconstruction of fundamental physics** based on a single ontological statement:

> **Axiom Po:**
> The universe is an unbounded 3D Euclidean energy continuum, in which
> **pressure, energy density, and energy per volume are identical**:
> ( P \equiv \epsilon )

From this starting point, the framework attempts to derive — within a single continuous medium — the structural foundations of:

* Classical mechanics
* Electromagnetism (Maxwell equations)
* Gravitation (Newtonian + Schwarzschild limit)
* Quantum structure (ℏ from vortex circulation quantization)
* Gauge symmetry (U(1) × SU(2) × SU(3) from topology)

### Why this may be worth your time

This is not a model extension or effective theory. It is a **reduction attempt**.

The framework makes several unusually strong claims:

* **No fundamental particles:** matter emerges as **topological vortex knots**
* **No separate pressure law:** pressure is *ontologically identical* to energy density
* **Field equations emerge geometrically:**
  Maxwell equations arise as identities of the shear field
* **Gravity is not curvature at the fundamental level:**
  it appears as a **buoyant response in an energy-density field**
* **ℏ is not postulated:** it emerges from a **circulation quantum of the medium**
